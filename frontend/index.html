About Us

At Bramways, we aim to provide families with peace of mind by providing an app that allows them to plan, monitor, and control the transit routes of their loved ones. Based in Brampton, Ontario, we are dedicated to making transportation simpler and safer for families commuting around the city. With Bramways, you can have confidence in a user-friendly app that empowers parents and caregivers with the information to stay in touch with children and elderly family members on their transit journeys.

Our app offers many features that will help no matter who you are, whether it be school drop-offs, extracurricular activities, or doctor’s appointments. Bramways offers you comfort in knowing that your family is safe during every transit experience. Features include optimized routes, real-time notifications, and live tracking. In today's world, we understand the importance of safety, community, and reliability, which is why Bramways is devoted to delivering a dependable solution that prioritizes your family's safety, making Brampton transit safer for everyone.
